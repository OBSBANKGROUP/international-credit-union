      Frequently Asked Questions

      How do I reset a user's password?

      Go to Manage Users, find the user, click Edit, enter a new password and save.

      How do I export user data?

      On the Users page, click the Export button to download a CSV of all registered members.

      How do I change the admin password?

      The admin credentials are set in the system configuration. Contact the dev team to update.

      What do the review statuses mean?

      Pending: awaiting review. Approved: account active. Suspended: account frozen.
