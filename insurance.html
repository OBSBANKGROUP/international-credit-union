Insurance Services

Protect yourself, your family, and your assets with comprehensive insurance solutions from International Credit Union.

Pet Insurance

Your pets are part of your family, and their health matters. Our pet insurance plans help cover veterinary costs, emergency treatments, medications, and routine check-ups. With flexible coverage options, you can ensure your pets receive the best possible care without worrying about unexpected expenses.

Whether you have a dog, cat, or other companion animal, our policies provide financial support for surgeries, diagnostics, and hospital stays so your pets can live long and healthy lives.

Business Insurance

Running a business involves risks, but the right protection can safeguard your investment. Our business insurance plans provide coverage for property damage, liability claims, employee-related risks, and unexpected interruptions.

Whether you operate a small startup or a large enterprise, our policies are designed to help protect your business operations, employees, and financial stability from unforeseen challenges.

Student Insurance

Students studying locally or abroad often face unexpected medical and travel expenses. Our student insurance plans provide affordable protection that covers healthcare, accidents, and emergency situations during your academic journey.

With international coverage options, students can focus on their education knowing they are protected from financial risks related to medical emergencies or travel disruptions.

Health Insurance

Healthcare costs can be unpredictable, but our health insurance plans ensure you and your family have access to quality medical services when you need them most.

Our coverage includes hospital visits, specialist care, prescriptions, preventive care, and emergency treatment so you can prioritize your health without financial stress.

Life Insurance

Life insurance provides financial security for your loved ones in the event of the unexpected. Our life insurance policies help ensure your family can manage expenses such as mortgages, education, and daily living costs.

With flexible policy options and long-term financial protection, you can plan for your family's future and provide peace of mind for generations to come.
